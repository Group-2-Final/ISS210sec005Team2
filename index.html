About

Gun violence in schools is a serious problem that has a devastating impact on students, families, and communities. According to data from the Gun Violence Archive, there were a total of 91 incidents of gun violence in schools in the United States in 2021. These incidents resulted in the deaths of 33 people, including 19 children. It is important to address gun violence in schools because it undermines the safety and well-being of students and can have long-term consequences for their mental health and general well-being. Additionally, school shootings can have a negative impact on the community at large, causing fear and anxiety among parents and other community members. There are a number of possible solutions to the problem of gun violence in schools. One approach is to increase funding for mental health services and support, which can help identify and address potential problems before they escalate. In addition, stricter gun control laws and regulations can help reduce the availability of firearms and make it more difficult for people who may be a threat to themselves or others to access them. Finally, implementing comprehensive safety and security measures in schools, such as metal detectors and security guards, can help prevent incidents of gun violence and protect students and staff.

Robb Elementary Victims

On Tuesday, May 24th, 2022 at Robb elementary school in Uvalde, Texas 19 4th graders and two teachers (not pictured) were brutally shot to death by 18-year-old Salvador Ramos. Ramos bought a semi-automatic rifle on his 18th birthday. The next day, he bought 375 rounds of ammunition from the same place, and two days later another semi-automatic rifle from the same location. Earlier in the day of the shooting, he had three facebook posts; the first saying he was going to kill his grandmother, the second that he killed his grandmother, and the third that he was going to "shoot up" a school. During the shooting, he wore a vest that is typically equipment for S.W.A.T. teams. The police have been highly criticized because despite arriving quickly to the scene, they would not enter the school while Ramos was shooting. Not pictured here are the children and teachers who witnessed this horror and could do nothing about it; they will live with this memory for the rest of their lives. The problem in this situation and many shootings is how Ramos or anyone needs access to semi-automatic weapons. They are not used in hunting, only murdering. The only people that should have access to this type of weapon are individuals who are in a position of protection such as Police or Miltitary personnel. Another question that comes up is why did the gun-shop owner not say anything, nor the girl that he was communicating with over facebook. We need to pay attention to these red flags as well as make gun laws far more strict if we do not want this horrible thing to happen again. https://nypost.com/2022/05/24/texas-school-shooting-live-updates/
